How It Works
1. Model Scoring

Production models score upcoming games across NFL, NCAA MBB, and MLB for home_win, cover_home, and total_over targets.

2. Edge Detection

Model probability is compared against market-implied probability (from moneyline odds). Bets require a minimum 3% edge.

3. Kelly Allocation

Quarter-Kelly criterion sizes each wager. No single bet exceeds 50% of the bankroll ($25 on a $50 bankroll).

4. Weekly Optimization

The weekly pipeline refits stale models (>14 days), then generates and stores the optimized betting plan.
